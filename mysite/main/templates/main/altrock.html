Alternative rock

Description:

Alternative Rock is a genre of rock music that emerged in the 1980s and became popular
in the 1990s. It originated as an alternative to mainstream rock, offering a diverse range
of sounds and styles that deviated from the conventions of traditional rock music.

Characterized by its nonconformist and independent spirit, alternative rock incorporates
elements from various genres such as punk rock, post-punk, grunge, indie rock, and more.
